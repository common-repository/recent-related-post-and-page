== Description ==

Recent Related Post And Page Plugin Display the post, page with beautifully designs and layouts. This plugin offer the 2 options to display the posts and page content, using shortcode & widget on your WordPress sites.

It offer the simple way to increase revenue and increase engagement on your WordPress Post, pages by helping your users find great related content using this Plugin!.  Related posts can increase your post views, help users find older content thatâ€™s still relevant, and keep your audience on your site for longer by helping them discover the right content at the right time

Using **[RRPP id=123]** shortcode, you can publish recent, related post & pages using plugin shortcode or widget feature. Simply copy & paste the plugin shortcode into any Page or Post in your WordPress sites.

You can activate recent and related posts/pages widget into any widget area.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `recent-related-post-and-page` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Widgets in Appearance Menu and activate widget.
